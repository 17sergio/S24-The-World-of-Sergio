Video Games

Video Games have existed every since I was little. I grew up with a Nintendo 64 and a Playstation 2. My fondest memories were playing with my dad when i was around 3 to 5 years old. I guess you could say I grew up with a controller in my hand ever since I was born. The games I was playing were NOT meant for 3 to 5 year olds to play but hey an adult was there and I had permission. To this day I still play video games it has become one of my favorites hobbies ever. Current games I love to play are Apex Legends, Grand Theft Auto 5, Valorant, Dragon Ball Z: Fighterz, and many more! Some of my fondest memories have been with a controller in my hand or me on desk with a mouse and keyboard playing video games.
